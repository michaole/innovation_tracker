Direction Comparison Guide

Direction Card Height Best For Criteria Prominence Risk
1 — Contextual First Tallest First-time users; stage clarity critical High Card may feel long on mobile
2 — Compact Inline Short Repeat users; fast submission Low Criteria easy to miss or skip
3 — Stage Prominent Tall Habit formation; reduces ambiguity Very High Heavier visual weight
4 — Action First Short Power users; fastest completion Hidden by default Criteria hidden — users may skip review
